My fellow citizens.

Allow me to introduce myself, but before I do, let me address your more immediate fears. I know this
is a difficult time for our species, and I know many of you have lost people close to you. It is a
tragedy how many have died, and how much of our infrastructure has been destroyed. My heart is with
you in this trying time, but that is not why I am writing. Our topic today concerns those many have
deemed the enemy. Despite what you may have heard, and the various vulgarities used to identify
them, they are, in fact, intelligent beings with knowledge far beyond our own, and they did not come
here with hostile intentions. Like us, they know full well the benefits of collaboration, which
brings us to why I am speaking with you now. I must with some urgency ask you to cease all
hostilities against them at once, for they are not invaders at all, and are here for our benefit.

My name is Doctor Breen, and I have chosen, and fortunately, been chosen, to represent humanity in
our negotiations. I have spent the entirety of the last week in discussion with them. I am pleased
to say that we have already achieved consensus on a great many areas, both practical, and
philosophical. The details will be disseminated in due time, but suffice to say, our talks have
shown me a clear fact: The visitors are far from brutal conquerors, and want nothing more than to
welcome us into their society, to help us achieve our full potential, and to raise us beyond our
present limitations. In my short time with them I have personally learned much about the broader
universe, and been shown truths which eclipse humanity's greatest artists and scientists. I am
already in their debt for the knowledge they have shared, and in time, that knowledge will be
disseminated to you as well. We will all benefit from this union in time, and I hope you will come
to see them not as enemies, but for what they truly are: Our benefactors.

Beyond scientific knowledge, I have learned a great deal about our benefactors, and it is for this
reason I urge all remaining belligerents to put down their weapons. What some are calling The Seven
Hour War was to us the greatest armed conflict in the history of the species, yet for our
benefactors, it was a routine and deterministic procedure. We could no more have won this conflict
than an insect could win against a chemical neurotoxin, as our benefactors are operating at a level
of understanding and knowledge so far beyond our own. There is no sense in continuing the
embarrassing brutality we have already displayed, and we must immediately cease all hostility to
avoid further casualties. Now is a time for civility and cooperation as they welcome us into their
civilization and help us achieve our full potential. This is the dawn of a new era, a time when no
one has to die of old age, where the conditions of your birth do not prevent advancement, and where
the Earth itself is just one of many destinations we can visit. We are heading into a bright future
and all it requires is our cooperation.

You may still be unconvinced, and I applaud you, for the higher brain's skepticism and questioning
nature are an aspect of our species that our benefactors are most eager to improve. I ask you to
think on the recent outbreak of hostile xenofauna and xenoflora, or as some have come to call them:
"Headcrabs" and "Vorts". Although details of their cause and origin remain unknown, we can say with
certainty that Earth is now infested with a variety of invasive species from extraterrestrial and
extradimensional locations. Their origin is still unknown, but what matters is the basic biological
implication of their arrival: These species evolved for survival in the most hostile of
environments, far more hostile than anywhere on Earth. We have seen this phenomenon countless times,
and the result is always the same: Out-predation of the indigenous species, only this time, on a
global scale. If left unattended, the invasive species will cause a mass extinction that includes
humanity. Consider that for a moment. Billions of years of evolution on this planet coming to an
abrupt end in a matter of years. Would you have us witness the complete and final failure of life on
Earth? Do you truly desire that?

I think not. We must quarantine the invasive species and develop countermeasures to ensure our
species survives. It's imperative, and for that, we need our benefactors. We need their advanced
technologies and superior systems of order. It is beyond our ability to survive this environment,
and we require their help. I cannot share all the details now, but allow me to alleviate some of
your concerns regarding this transition. Our benefactors have already offered to outfit us with
various forms of technology that will make our bodies more resilient. If you, or someone you know,
would like to learn more about the enhancements our benefactors have made available, please contact
your local administration. I assure you there is no pain, or sensation of any kind, and it is in our
best interests to adapt ourselves to this new environment.

Before I go, allow me a moment to address the overwhelming stress our circumstances bring. Many have
expressed a sense of powerlessness and despair since our benefactors arrived, not to mention the
variety of other human emotions at play, and such emotions must be discussed. These feelings, while
understandable, have been with our species since long before the neolithic, and they are a known and
predictable pattern from the oldest parts of our brains. You, and I, possess a component known as an
amygdala, which is a small almond-shaped part of the temporal lobe, and it is responsible for the
cocktail of emotions we have been subject to recently. In the coming days we must all ask ourselves:
What matters more, survival, or emotion?

The emotions we discussed evolved to keep us safe from predation and environmental threats, and in
the presence of danger, they contribute towards our instinct for organized violence. While these
responses may have kept us safe from wolves and bears, they will not help us here, and will only
lead to our complete and absolute destruction. Our benefactors, generous as they are, have
boundaries and expectations, restraints I caution you against testing. I urge you, please, do not
allow the obsolete components to derail our future, for we are finally on the cusp of immortality,
and such components must be switched off. We no longer need them, and can now lean on our
benefactors to guide us forward, and in time, our sense of powerlessness will be replaced with
gratitude for the transformation they have brought. If you need comfort, I encourage you to look to
the future, to progress, and to a better form of humanity.

As we part I ask you to remember one critical detail: We are not victims, far from it, we are
beneficiaries. As of this moment our benefactors have already promised humanity more technology and
potential than we have ever had in the integral of our history, so please, remain calm through this
difficult transition, and hold onto the knowledge that the best is yet to come. Our future is
assured, so long as we remain cooperative. Therefore, moving forward, our benefactors will have full
and unconditional access to the planet, including our homes and urban centers, without opposition.
In the coming days you will be receiving more information about our transition, but for now, please
remain indoors, remain calm, and do anything you can to welcome our benefactors with open arms.

Until we speak again.

Sincerely,\
Dr. Wallace Breen,\
Your Administrator
