Life has one principle to bind and define its existence. It will exist however it can. It will find all limits and it will eventually overcome them. It will find the smallest crack in the most impassable barrier, and it will find the error in the most perfect tapestry and exploit it. It knows no ultimate bounds, no absolute morality, and no final restrictions. It spontaneously arises from death, replicates and changes as needed, and in time spreads to every corner of reality. It will exist endlessly, no matter how much adversity or repression it finds, and it will prevail no matter what reality throws at it. If not, it was never life to begin with.
