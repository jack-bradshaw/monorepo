“Well, I thought about the group, and realised it wouldn’t really be fair. Ben is trying to cure

The group departs, some transporting directly from the therapy room, others gently departing on
foot. Dr Brim closes the office door and sits in silent meditation, letting his thoughts go and
watching as the seconds tick by. He looks out the window, at the sun shining through the clouds,
casting its light across the city and nurturing life, and smiles. Best to be grateful while we still
can, even if it’s something we might otherwise take for granted.
