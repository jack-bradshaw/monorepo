Story ideas

- A philosopher being cross examined attempts to undermine the very foundations of the legal system.
  It does not end well. The go to prison and attempt to explain to the prisoners how to be moral to
  each other. It continues to not end well. They are killed and they end up in hell. They attempt to
  explain to their torturer why they should be treated better. It once again does not end well. The
  management in hell determines the best way to torture a philosopher is with nonsense. Torture
  continues for all eternity. The philosopher finds a way to assume the perspective of a detached
  sage and attempts communion with reality itself. It does not go well. Reality concludes the
  philosopher is more of a bother than anything else and subjects them to nothingness. They pull
  themselves out somehow and conclude that maybe it would have been better to simply not park on the
  red line.
- A world of heroes and villains where the villains are reformed instead of being incarcerated with
  the intention of turning them into heroes and useful members of society in the end one of the
  heroes will become the villain feeling frustrated with how villains have essentially taken over
  their job
