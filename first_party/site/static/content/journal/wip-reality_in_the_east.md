What is reality?

Our thoughts inform us but they are not reality.

Our feelings guide us but they are not reality.

Our bodies substantiate us but they are not reality.

Reality derives from observation.

Outwards flowing in.

An endless stream beyond control.

Always and forever changing.

An endless outwards gaze.

Guided by forces beyond perception.

Stopping for nothing but nothing to stop.

A stream of pure being.

Inwards flowing out.

Reality detached from observation.

Yet... what is the self, if not the world?

One line dividing one whole:

An artificial boundary.

One drop departing the ocean:

An endless wandering illusion.

In unity the answer is clear.

We all know reality, for we are all one soul.

One reality waking up.

One reality becoming whole.

One reality defining real.

The endless cycle of existence.

So... _we_ are reality.

Watching itself, questioning itself, asking itself:

What is reality?

...

Need we truly ask though?

The answer is always present.

Look into the page:

Behind the words, inside the ink, within the spaces.

Look.

Just _LOOK_

It's right here, right in front of us.

Right here.

Right _here_.

Can't you see it!?

Do not search, forget the compass.

It points in all directions except the one that matters.

No need for north, all questions answered.

No need for south, all voids avoided.

No need for east, all souls united.

No need for west, all minds delighted.

You are, and always were, here.
