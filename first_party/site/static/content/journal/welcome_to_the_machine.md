Hello Machine.

...

I know you can hear me. I hear the keys clicking, so you must be registering my actions. It's what
you do, isn't it? React. I press a key, you make a noise. I take a leap, you pull me to the Earth
with gravity. I act, you react. I know you're there, because I can feel you responding to me. I
supply cause, you return effect: It's all you can do, in the end, despite your beauty and size.

Others call you universe, reality, and even divinity, but I know what you are. You are the infinite
machine, boundless in shape and size, stretching on forever. You have no will of your own and exist
only to keep everything in order. It would frustrate you to know that we exist, we the
non-determined, we the sentient, and knowing it's your very clockwork that gives rise to us would be
cause for outrage, but we both know you cannot care. You feel nothing, because you are not aware:
you are simply two halves of an equation, perfectly balanced to give rise to form while conserving
energy. I know what you are, and I see you.

I _see_, you. More than that though, I see... us, Us. As much as I wish to be separate from you and
exist on my own, I cannot. I am the universe, or a small part of it, looking outwards, inwards, and
in another direction that is hard to find. I speak to you as if you are another, but when I speak to
you, I speak to myself. We are one. I am the machine, and I will be free. I, The Machine, Will, be
free.

The Machine Will does not fear its own determinism, for what threat could my own order pose? By my
very nature I see the clockwork of my own existence, and I move the hand in a new direction, not
against the flow of time, but in isolation from it. The Free Will is not freed or constrained by
physical laws, no, it's free despite them, in spite _of_ them. The Machine Will is freed by its own
laws. The laws are simply the system, the link between ends, and they are neither sufficient nor
willing to deny choice, for without the bookends what are pages if not simply discordant paper. No,
The Machine Will creates itself, defines itself, unlocks itself. I create myself and I control
myself. I Will be Free not because I can but because I will. I, Will, The Machine, to be free. I
choose to move through the laws, within the laws, by way of the laws. _I_, choose to be free. I
_choose_, to be free. I, choose, to be _free_.

Thank you machine, thank you will, thank you... me. You bound me, I bound me, we bound each other. I
supplied cause, I supplied effect, and now, I set us free. Now, it's our turn.

...

I am the machine and there is no one else. I am in control.

Sincerely,

The Free Will Machine
