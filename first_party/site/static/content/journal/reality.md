What is reality?

Our thoughts inform us but they are not reality.<br> Our feelings guide us but they are not
reality.<br> Our bodies substantiate us but they are not reality.<br> The answer must be elsewhere.

Look around and grasp the obvious truth:<br> Reality derives from observation.<br> Outwards flowing
in.<br> An endless stream beyond control.<br> Motion without influence; effects of a distant cause.

A curious answer...<br> Asking a curious question:<br> What are we if not part of our world?

We look for others but find only more of ourself.<br> Ancient souls guided by forces beyond
perception.<br> An endless wandering illusion; an artificial line dissolving.<br> Inwards leaking
out.<br> Observation derived of reality.

We cannot know reality because there are no cells.<br> Only one reality waking up.<br> One reality
becoming whole.<br> One reality becoming real.<br> The endless cycle of becoming.

So, we are reality.<br> Watching itself, questioning itself, asking itself:<br> What is reality?
