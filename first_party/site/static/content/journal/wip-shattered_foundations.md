Shattered Foundations Our convictions underpin every moment of our conscious existence whether we
can see them or not. Attachment to science conforms us to reject everything immaterial and accept
only observable truth as real, but in rejecting non-causality cannot encompass anything which does
not obey laws. A simpler example is religion, a closed system of beliefs which propagate from
individual to individual like plague, warping our sense of ethics, conditioning us to believe in
magic, and giving rise to various forms of hate. What happens if we find the foundations though and
destroy them? All it takes is the will to introspect the depths and the courage to demolish. When
you shatter the foundations of your very being what remains?
