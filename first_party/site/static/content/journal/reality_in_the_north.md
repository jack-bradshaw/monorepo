What is reality?

Why stop there?

Where is reality?

How is reality?

Why is reality?

Who made it so, and is it even real?

Questions. Endless questions.

Enough to fill itself.

Enough to grow the mind.

Enough to wake the soul.

Yet for all its beauty and all its treasures:

Perhaps reality is just a curiosity:

An endless mystery.

A reminder to ask questions.

A reason to seek greater heights.

Despite our cunning we may never find an answer...

So tell me, as you ascend in search of truth:

What would you do with an answer?

Where would you go next?

And most importantly:

Why do you want to know?
