The Refined vs the Raw Most of my writing undergoes significant revision before publication to the
degree that my original work rarely mirrors what others see. It is a necessary process, but it
leaves me feeling somewhat empty. My original work often holds beauty which will go unseen. I wonder
if there is some way to show others my work in both raw and refined forms, so that neither is lost.
Would others enjoy reading both perhaps? I am going to experiment with this once I have more
readers. I will keep everything raw, for now release only the refined material, and one day consider
whether others would like to see the raw content.
