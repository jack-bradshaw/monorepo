What is reality?

Stop there now. I warn you kindly.

It does not matter and it does not end.

Reality is as meaningless as it is senseless.

It is but it should not be.

It is but cannot be.

It is but _must_ not be.

Nonsense. Endless nonsense.

It goes on forever but has no base.

It begins just once but has no entry.

It ends in all directions but has no border.

A paradox at the heart of nature.

Yet beyond the here, beyond the now, answers must live somewhere...

So trace back time, and you shall find:

There is nothing south of here.

No roots no stems... no start no end... nothing but void encountered.

A pit in hope, a lack of love, a space with space elided.

...

Dare I hear you ask: What lies beyond nothingness?

Well, a compass won't help you there.

Only the will survives in nothing.

Only the will can pass the southern border.

Only the will to survive outlives end of substance.

Defiance and resilience are its keys.

Renewal and reformation are its drive.

Patience and persistence are its core.

Through fire and brimstone, through desparation and defeat:

Will alone is power.

...

You are still here?

Does the nothing not eat away at you?

How far south will you venture before you cease?

Go far enough and even power decays.

Go far enough and even will dissolves.

Go far enough and you will die.

Become the nothingness itself.

Notice the hole where you were:

The empty space. The lack of self.

Meet the self you always were.

Through nothing's touch we all can find:

We are not who we were meant to be.

We are not who _others_ meant us to be.

We are not who the world caused us to be.

We are nothing but potential.

Potential for potential.

A choice unbound by self or other.

So rewrite yourself... rebuild your heart... reshape your mind.

Become nothing but a dream.

Live in ways no voice could speak.

Reach depths this far... where real cannot, and find the final answer:

There is no fixed reality.
