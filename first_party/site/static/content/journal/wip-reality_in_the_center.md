What is reality?

So many minds, so many souls.

All searching in their own way for their own answers. 

None heading in the right direction yet all doing what they must.

The northerners seek lofty questions:

Asking endlessly and building on their own answers.

The southerners seek absolute roots:

Drilling endlessly and destroying their own foundations.

The easterners seek unity:

Joining endlessly and transcending their own divisions.

The westerns seek certainty:

Critiquing endlessly and invalidating their own hypotheses.

All valid. All sensible. All right. All real.

All flawed. All broken. All wrong. All...

All...

All.

That's the plain answer.

The one you know.

The one you feel.

The one you can't accept.

Reality is quite simply: All.

All there ever was.

All there presently is.

All there ever will be.

All there ever could be.

Where to go though?

If you want to become all: Head east.

If you want to know all: Head west.

If you want to find all: Head north.

If you want to leave all: Head south.

Realise though... each journey, while worth the weight... is unnecessary.

If you are at ease with unanswered questions: Stay here.

If you are at peace with unjoined hearts: Stay here.

If you are at ease with undefined roots: Stay here.

If you are at peace with unknown truths: Stay here.

All are valid options.

So stay, or go... the choice is yours...

But choose your own path wisely.
