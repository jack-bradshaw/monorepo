Moral Constraint The moral constraint is the human need to follow some system of morality but not
necessarily a particular system. Some follow doctrines, others follow a more dynamic approach. In
any case, human beings all have the need to follow some morality.

Why though. Can a person exist without morality? Or perhaps is morality a prerequisite for
personhood? If so can a person lose their personhood by losing their morality?

All good questions, with many answers but no facts. Just perspectives and opinions.
