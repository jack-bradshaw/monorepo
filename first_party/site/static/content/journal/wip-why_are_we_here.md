why are we here? i cannot explain it, no matter how hard I try. I look around me and see a universe
which should by all reason not exist. i see solid walls, feel the pull of gravity, observe the
electromagnetic force, and witness the dynamic interplay of the forces of nature, but cannot for the
life of me explain why reality exists. i can see light and seek the source, but following the thread
of causality recursively reveals only an infinite regress of cause and effect leading further into a
past I cannot touch. through science i can learn the material ways the universe and extrapolate then
backwards, but our theories only explain the how, not the why, and none of them are capable of
returning to the ultimate source of existence. if we are without origin, then we are eternal, and if
we have origin, then we have only more questions. why... _why_ are we here?
