# Pain

I find it difficult to rationalise the universe’s ability to experience pain, but as a living part
of the universe the reality of this truth is as apparent to me as any truth can be. If we zoom out
all the way to the edges of existence and look inwards at everything that is, all across the
universe, presumably, are pain centres. Life forms coming into being, experiencing existence, and
evolving a pain response to avoid danger and remain alive. What substance could possibly have the
fundament property of being capable of experiencing pain. It’s such a strange universe this one.
