Identify a problem. Any problem. Your neighbours loud music. Higher prices at the gas station than
last week. Late pizza delivery. Global warming. A few things in our control but most of them are
not. Some phenomena in this world are so far beyond anyone’s control. If you trace any problem back
far enough you’ll often find culture. A cultural issue causing people to behave a particular way,
act a particular way, or speak a particular way. Some people try to change culture but you can’t. No
one can. At least not top down. You can distort it and convolute it with policy but it always finds
a way back to its natural form. It’s like a river, flowing endlessly through humanity. It influences
us all but is beyond our influence. We are completely out of control.
