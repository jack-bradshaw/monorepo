I am no longer who I once was. I want to explore that because it holds
a new perspective on the truth. In years past my mind was fixed firmly on the objective world, the
place of facts and reason, but in recent years it has shifted to accommodate the subjective world
within us. In this change a radical new perspective has presented itself to me. I looked at myself
and saw someone who hated humanity, not overtly, but implicitly, and it was corrupting my life and
the lives of the people closest to me. I have come to realise that human beings are not rational,
and that we are a deeply flawed species. In order to relate to others of my own kind, it was
necessary to deeply refactor myself and make space for this messiness. All this to say, it has been
transformation of my deepest beliefs about humanity. We are beautiful because we are not objective.
